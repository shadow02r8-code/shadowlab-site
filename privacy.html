Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Shadow Lab ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our services, including ReplyFlow.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Email address and password when you register
  • TikTok account data: TikTok user ID, username, display name, and avatar URL obtained via TikTok OAuth
  • TikTok access tokens: OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens required to operate automations on your behalf
  • Usage data: Automation rules you create, keywords you configure, and logs of DMs sent through our service
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and usage analytics for service improvement

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate our automation services
  • Authenticate your TikTok account and execute automations on your behalf
  • Send DMs to TikTok users who trigger your configured keyword rules
  • Monitor and prevent abuse of our services
  • Communicate with you about your account and our services
  • Improve and develop new features

3. TikTok Data

When you connect your TikTok account, we access and store your TikTok user information and OAuth tokens. This data is used solely to operate automations on your behalf. We access TikTok APIs in accordance with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service and API usage policies.

We do not sell, share, or use your TikTok data for advertising purposes. TikTok access tokens are stored securely and used only to perform the actions you have explicitly configured.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers: We use Supabase for database hosting and Vercel for application hosting. These providers process data on our behalf under appropriate data protection agreements.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal process.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. DM send logs are retained for up to 90 days. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your information
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@shadowlab.site.

8. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain your session and for security purposes (e.g., OAuth state verification). We do not use tracking cookies for advertising.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at info@shadowlab.site.
